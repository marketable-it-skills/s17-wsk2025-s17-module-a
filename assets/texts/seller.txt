Benefits for sellers:

1. An audience looking for exactly what you offer  
Our platform targets buyers of collectible and themed goods — from LEGO to figurines. Your listing will be seen by people already interested in buying.

2. Free listing placement  
You can publish items with no hidden commissions or fees for basic placement.

3. Simple, fast publishing  
An intuitive interface lets you add a listing in a couple of minutes — upload photos, describe the item, and you’re done.

4. Flexible listing settings  
Set the price, delivery terms, preferred contact method, and highlight your listings for greater reach.

5. Support for the hobby community  
You’re not just selling an item — you’re finding it a new appreciator among like-minded people.

6. Opportunity for ongoing income  
If you resell, customize, or assemble themed lots, you’ll find a steady audience here.

7. No competition with “mass market” listings  
No furniture, appliances, or off-topic goods — only niche collectible categories.
