Every collectible treasure deserves a new home.
Decided to part with a LEGO set, a figurine, or a rare comic? Our platform helps you do it quickly, conveniently, and safely.

Step 1: Register in 1 minute
It starts with a simple step — creating an account. You only need an email and a name. No extra data, no bureaucracy.

Step 2: Create a listing
Click “Publish”, add photos, describe the item, choose a category and price. An intuitive form guides you through everything you need. It takes just 2–3 minutes.

Step 3: Promotion (optional)
Want to sell faster? Use paid options: highlight your listing with color, pin it to the top, or feature it on the home page. Flexible rates — from one day up.

Step 4: Talk to buyers
Receive messages directly in your account. Notifications go to your email — no interest goes unnoticed.

Step 5: Deal and delivery
Agree on terms with the buyer — in person or with delivery. The review system builds trust and helps shape your reputation.

Step 6: The story continues
Your item has a new owner. And you — new experience and free space for your next collectible finds.
