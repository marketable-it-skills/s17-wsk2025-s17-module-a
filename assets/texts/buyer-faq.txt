Question: What is this even for?
Answer: Platforms like this help people find the items they need directly from sellers. It’s convenient, fast, and often cheaper than in stores.

Question: How does it all work?
Answer: You find an item you’re interested in, contact the seller, discuss the details, then agree on handover and payment. It’s simple and straightforward.

Question: Is it safe?
Answer: As with any deal between individuals, caution matters. Don’t pay in advance, inspect the item in person at pickup, and trust your instincts.

Question: What if I get scammed?
Answer: Unfortunately, risk exists everywhere. To minimize it, use only trusted contact methods, meet in public places, and don’t agree to suspicious terms.

Question: What if I change my mind?
Answer: It happens. It’s best to discuss the possibility of canceling the deal with the seller upfront and agree on everything before money or goods change hands.

Question: Why is it cheaper than in a store?
Answer: Because you buy directly from a person, without middlemen or markups. Often these are pre-owned items or things someone no longer needs, sold below market price.
