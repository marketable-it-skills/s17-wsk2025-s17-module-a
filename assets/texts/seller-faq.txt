Question: Who even needs this?
Answer: People for whom your item or service is genuinely useful. You’d be surprised how many things sit unused while someone is already looking for exactly that.

Question: How does it work?
Answer: You post a listing with a description and photos. People see it, contact you, and you agree on handover and payment. Simple and to the point.

Question: What if nobody responds?
Answer: The main thing is a good description and a fair price. Interest usually appears fairly quickly, especially if the item is in demand.

Question: How do I sell faster?
Answer: Quality photos, an honest description, a reasonable price, and quick replies — that’s the recipe for success. People value courtesy and openness.

Question: Is it safe?
Answer: If you follow basic precautions — yes. Meet in busy places, don’t hand over the item before payment, and don’t agree to odd terms.

Question: What if the buyer changes their mind?
Answer: It happens. Just move on — someone who is genuinely interested usually appears soon after.
